OVERVIEW

Project Background
Johnson & Johnson is requesting assistance from Microsoft to help them transform their Quality Management System (QMS) to be a touchless experience by 2023.
QueSt will support Johnson & Johnson with their Quality Systems and Post Market Quality by:
• Engaging in customer experience initiatives that will continue to put their patients and customers at the center of what they do.

• Optimize and standardize for better responsiveness and improved efficiency while ensuring robust compliance

  • Bot Personality
The personality of chatbot was used to be overlooked, but it is one of the most important points for building a ideal user experience. The personality will affect the conversation, and the conversation will affect the user experience.
I made 8 personality cards for the engineer managements to choose in the workshop, and the personality "Helper" had most of the likes.
After the main personality is decided, we can deep dive into tone of voice which will determine how the bot will sound like if it's integrated properly.
  • Bot Look
The look and feel for chatbot is mainly about the profile image and the whole ui style. In a toB project, the look and feel will be affected by the customer brand guideline. In this case, simple mark type profile image is determined by the customer, and the brand red color of Johnson and Johnson is applied

  • Conversational UX
The conversational UX is the most complex part in the whole process, however, only the previous steps are confirmed and finished, and then we can move to the conversational part.

Two perosnas are included in the product issue escalation process, QueSt will recognize the login user type and start related conversation based on the role.

1. Quality Engineer

To initiate an escalation, the Quality Engineer reviews Part, Product and Lot details which QueSt automatically populates.

Then Using Machine Learning technology in Microsoft Azure, the Quality Engineer is able to initiate an escalation through Quest by evaluating the most relevant triggers which are automatically recommended based on the Johnson and Johnson defined list of quality criteria.

The quality Engineer can review and select from the list or, if needed, review the full list of criteria

2. Escalation Engineer

Then the submitted escalation is received by the Escalation Engineer who then reviews pre-populated issue details, the result of QueSt integrating with Source Applications such as REACT, Windchill and others.

For example, to escalate a medical and safety related issue, the escalation engineer reviews and creates a Product Issue Escalation record and Product Issue Assessment record in creating an agenda for the RMB meeting decision. QueSt machine learning component and algorithms are continuously retrained using Key Words to recommend Continuous Action and Preventive action records for FDA compliance.
