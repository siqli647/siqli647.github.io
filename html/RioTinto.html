OVERVIEW

Project Background
To prevent accidents and increase safety awareness among front-line teams, Rio Tinto requires all staff to complete safety control verifications before beginning work at mining sites.

The application is implemented on PowerApps Canvas for front-line workers to go through different verification forms based on their location, language and working type.

PROBLEM

UX Workshop
After the initial workshop, we conducted a follow-up deep dive workshop with the front-line team as our target user group. This workshop allowed us to further explore their needs, finalize the user journey map, and determine the final deliverables from a UX perspective.

    UAT

    Design Validation

    Design validation is a critical process that helps Rio Tinto to ensure the quality, safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction of FST Application.


    Based on UES model (User Experience System from Alibaba Cloud), we measured FST from 5 dimensions: Satisfaction; Ease of Use; Consistency; Task Efficiency and Performance

    Ease of Use

    Product performance feedbacks collected through questionnaire based on SUS

    Consistency

    Overall style, components, visual language and interactions evaluated based on Walkthorugh

    Satisfaction

    Level of user satisfaction collected through rate function in FST app

    Performance

    API request response time, CVV loading time and page request response time

    Tasks

    Tasks completion rate, time and utilization level

    Final Score

    As promised in the SOW (Statement of Work), a score higher than 3.5 will be considered as a satisfied deliverable accepted by the customer. Without any doubts, the product hits the target.
